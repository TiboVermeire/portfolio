Rit

Rit is a platform dedicated to providing a solution for people with limited mobility to access public transportation. By engaging volunteers, Rit helps these individuals reach their destinations safely and conveniently. The platform ensures flexibility and ease, enabling everyone to continue participating in daily life.

HomeEase

MMake caregiving worry-free with HomeEase: HomeEase is a platform designed for families providing care to loved ones. It helps them easily divide tasks and keep track of what needs to be done and who has already completed specific tasks. In essence, it's a caregiving agenda that brings clarity and organization to the responsibilities of caregiving..

Menu maker

Introducing the Menu Maker for restaurants: a website designed to simplify the menu creation process with an intuitive, user-friendly interface. This menu configurator allows restaurant owners to effortlessly design and customize their menus, incorporating optional nudging techniques to guide them through the process. With features that streamline layout design and content management, the Menu Maker ensures that creating a professional and appealing menu is both easy and efficient.

JoyArt

JoyArt is an innovative prototype of an AR-powered app designed for museums to enhance visitor experiences. Using augmented reality technology, JoyArt allows users to engage deeply with exhibits by providing immersive, interactive content and detailed information about the artworks and artifacts in their immediate surroundings. This cutting-edge tool enriches museum visits, offering a dynamic way to explore and learn about current exhibitions in a more engaging and informative manner.
